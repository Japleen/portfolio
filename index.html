About Me

Hey, I am Japleen. By profession, I'm a full stack web developer, working with technology every day. But I'm also an artist at heart. I love to dance when I'm happy, listen to music or sing when I'm feeling emotional, paint when I'm feeling creative or become a self proclaimed pianist, and write with heart to share my thoughts with others. I'm always striving to create an impact and make the world a better place in my own way because it has shaped us into who we are. I believe in leaving the world better than I found it.

I'm trying to find a good balance in life, making sure I'm healthy and happy while I work. I work hard, party harder and priortize health, rest and relationships. I really believe in taking care of myself and my loved ones, like eating well, keeping clean and exercising regularly. It's important for me to feel good inside and out, so I spend time on things like eating right, dressing well, practicing yoga and reconnecting with myself and my loved ones.

I find solace and answers to all my questions and prayers in the Gurbani of 'Dhan Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji' and strive to practice gratitude, daswandth, and other teachings.

I feel grateful to have a loving and caring family, a group of supportive and proud people always believing in me and helping me nurture and grow.

I explore and review places, food, and products that are worth experiencing. I enjoy styling, accessorizing, and rejuvenating my skin and hair using nature's boundless treasures.

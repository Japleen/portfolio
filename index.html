About Me

Hey, I am Japleen. I'm always trying to find a good balance in life, making sure I'm healthy and happy while I work. I really believe in taking care of myself and my loved ones, like eating well and keeping clean. It's important for me to feel good inside and out, so I spend time on things like eating right and looking after myself and my loved ones.

By profession, I'm a full stack web developer, working with technology every day. But I'm also an artist at heart. I love to dance when I'm happy, listen to music when I'm feeling emotional, paint when I'm feeling creative or become a self proclaimed pianist, and write to share my thoughts with others. I'm always trying to make the world a better place in my own way.

I explore and review places, food, and products that are worth experiencing. I enjoy styling, accessorizing, and rejuvenating my skin and hair using nature's boundless treasures.
